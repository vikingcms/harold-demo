Seriously Scary Good Books

Created February 6, 2020

The Goosebumps books written by R.L. Stine were some seriously scary good books. Classics likeSay Cheese and Die, andWelcome to Deadhouseare just among a few.

No matter the season, you can always rely on a good Goosebumps tale to raise the hair on your arms and intrigue your cerebrum to continue reading.

Many other classics include Stay out of the Basement, Welcome to Camp Nightmare, and Return of the Mummy.

These classic books from the 90’s captivated the youth and helped many kids begin their journey of reading.
